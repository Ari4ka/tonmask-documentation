TonMask wallet is MetaMask analog for The Open Network. TON blockchain has different interfaces with rest of blockchains, which makes it impossible to adapt existing wallets to use it. TonMask web extension wallet have the same architecture as a MetaMask, but build in a modern stack and with more functional approach.

#### Store, send tokens

Available as a browser extension, TonMask equips you with a key vault, secure login, token wallet.

#### Explore blockchain apps

TonMask provides the simplest yet most secure way to connect to blockchain-based applications.
You are always in control when interacting on the new decentralized web.

#### Own your data

TonMask generates passwords and keys on your device, so only you have access to your accounts and data.
You always choose what to share and what to keep private.

## Features

Simplify you way to use blockchain technology.

- A key vault for **multiple** wallets
- Switch Mainnet/Testnet in on click
- Zero-commission transactions
- Support **jetons**
- Inpage TON provider
- DApps **whitelist**
- Fiat balance
