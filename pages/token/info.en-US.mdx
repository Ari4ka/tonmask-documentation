## What is OpenMask Token

OPNT is a jetton by TEP-89 (Jetton Wallet Discovery) standard on The Open Network (TON).

OPNT is utility token for OpenMask wallet and future OpenProduct projects. Jetton has an specific feature
to proxy messages to implement utility functions.

- Network: The Open Network (TON)
- Total supply: 21,000,000 OPNT
- Mutable: No
- Contract Address: ABC
- Source code: [GitHub](https://github.com/OpenProduct/openmask-token)
